📖 About Bibliography Cleaner

A tool for lazy researchers who copy thousands of entries but only use 1% of them

What does this tool do?

Bibliography Cleaner helps you remove unused citation entries from your .bib files. This is especially useful for academic writers who maintain large bibliography databases but only cite a small portion of the entries in any given document.

How it works:

  1. Analysis: Use the checkcites tool to analyze your LaTeX document and identify unused citations
  2. Upload: Upload your .bib file and the unused keys file to our web app
  3. Processing: Our tool processes your files and removes all unused entries
  4. Download: Get your cleaned .bib file with only the citations you actually use

Benefits:

  • 🎯 Cleaner documents: Only include references you actually cite
  • ⚡ Faster compilation: Smaller .bib files compile faster
  • 📝 Better organization: Keep your bibliography focused and relevant
  • 💾 Reduced file size: Smaller files are easier to manage and share

Privacy & Security:

Your files are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers. All uploaded files are automatically deleted after processing.

Technical Details:

This tool is based on a Python script that parses checkcites output and filters bibliography entries accordingly. It preserves the structure of your .bib file while removing only the unused entries.
